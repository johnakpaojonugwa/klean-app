Toast Notifications Preview

Interactive demo of the Klean Frontend toast system

Toast Types

Success Toast (3 seconds)

Used for successful operations, confirmations, and completed actions.

Error Toast (Persistent)

Used for critical errors that require user acknowledgment.

Warning Toast (7 seconds)

Used for validation errors, alerts, and warnings.

Info Toast (4 seconds)

Used for informational messages and general announcements.

Multiple Toasts & Stacking

Maximum 3 visible toasts at once with automatic stacking.

Duration Reference

✓ Success Toast: 3 seconds (auto-dismisses)

✗ Error Toast: Persistent (requires manual close)

⚠ Warning Toast: 7 seconds (auto-dismisses)

ℹ Info Toast: 4 seconds (auto-dismisses)

Max Visible: 3 toasts at once (oldest will hide when new ones appear)
